About BET


Have you ever wondered where your money went because it seemed that it suddenly disappeared?


BET will help you stop thinking where your money went as it will help you track every spending. Not only that, it will also help you start telling where your money should go. Sounds exciting?, Knowing how much you earn and how much you spend will tell you how much you can potentially save. expense trackers is to help you not just track your expenses, they’re also designed to help you evaluate if your money was spent wisely so you can increase your savings and investments. By the end of every month, evaluate your spending. Did you spend your money for essentials or you could have saved some more? BET has created daily, weekly and monthly expense trackers for you.Begin by listing the item and the amount you’ve spent. Next, evaluate if the your purchase was a need or merely a want.Based on last month’s expense tracker, you now have an idea on how you can potentially increase your savings for the following month. Remember the formula for success: Earnings-Savings= Expenses So next month, don’t just track your spending. Set an ideal budget for each purchase you make. We know, budgeting is one of those things that’s quiet challenging to do. But it’s something that needs to be done in order to allocate our money wisely so we can decide which purchases we can afford based on our financial limitations.
